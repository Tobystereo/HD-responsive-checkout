    Privacy Policy

    Privacy is important to us

    Because we know it's important to you

    We try and keep our policies simple and straight forward. So this page covers two primary, yet basic issues. One talks about how we will handle your personal information.

    The other lets you know that we will always use absolute discretion — to ensure your privacy — in our packaging and phone communications with you.

    How we obtain, use and store your personal information

    Our website (www.hairdirect.com) may ask you for a variety of personal details at various places. Or maybe as a prerequisite to performing a specific action such as our sweepstakes, contact forms, e-brochures, newsletters or online store.

    We will only keep this information permanently with your explicit permission — or if you make a hair system or product purchase in our store.

    Under no circumstances will we ever sell, lease, loan or rent your information to any other company for any reason whatsoever. You're our customer... and we won't share!

    How we communicate and send packages to you

    We will never make a cold sales call to you for any reason. All personal phone and email communications will be at your request, or meant only to conduct specific business that relates to your order or account with us.

    When we call you, our representative will only identify our company as "HD Inc" or "HD" — unless they are speaking directly with you. Also, all of our postal mail and shipped packages are discreet and will identify our company as "HD Inc" or "HD".

    Our Email Newsletter — We have an informative newsletter that is emailed every month to our subscribers. It's called Buzz Mail and we have a strict SPAM-FREE policy that ensures this newsletter will only be sent to you at your request.

    It's important to note that due to the nature of this e-newsletter, and the inherent privacy of email as a whole, we do identify ourselves as "Hair Direct" and openly discuss topics related to hair loss and hair replacement.

    That about sums it up. If you have any questions about any of our company or privacy policies. Please don't hesitate to contact us or call toll-free @ 1-800-424-7436.

    Shipping Policy

    Shipping Costs & Policies

    We ship hair and accessory products differently. That’s because they are very different products. For example, custom hair systems take several weeks to create, and they come to our US office before they’re sent to you. Accessories (shampoos, tapes, etc) are different. They can be purchased from different stores and are shipped soon after you order them. 

    Discreet Packaging for Your Privacy

    We take your privacy seriously! The contents and source of your order will not appear on the delivered package. Every hair and accessories order is shipped in a plain box, labeled “HD Inc.” on the shipping tag.

    Courier Options

    We have a variety of shipping methods available, depending on the urgency of your order. US and global shipping methods include:

    • U.S. Postal Service
    • UPS

    More shipping options are available upon request

    PLEASE NOTE: To help save you money, we might occasionally change the shipping method you select during checkout if we know an alternative type of shipping would get your package to you just as quickly, but for less money. 

    How We Ship Hair

    After your hair replacement system arrives from the factory, we'll ship it right out to you, provided there is no outstanding unpaid balance on your account. Our hair system shipping rates are based on a flat scale for most locations: $12.95 for delivery within the continental US; $36.00 outside the continental US.

    All custom hair orders are shipped globally from our US & Worldwide Store. A signature will be required for delivery, unless you request that no signature be required.

    In the US & Worldwide store, men’s stock hair orders must be placed by 12pm EDT to qualify for same day shipping. If ordered together, men’s Stocks and accessory products will ship together.

    Production Times for Custom-Made Hair Systems

    Top of head (partial) hair systems usually require 8 weeks to create and full cap systems will take about 12-14 weeks. Typical design and manufacturing times will vary, because each system is hand-made, and some are more complicated to produce than others. 

    Super Rush Orders

    We can place a Super Rush on custom hair system orders for an additional cost. A partial hair system with a Super Rush will be delivered 35 days from the order date; full cap systems take only 45 days. Super Rushes are guaranteed: if we miss the delivery date, we’ll refund the additional cost. Contact us for details, limited availability.

    How We Ship Accessories

    All of our shipping costs for accessory products are calculated at the time of your order, according to the weight of your shipment, type of delivery you choose and your location. Payment is taken when the order is shipped.

    All product orders received prior to 2pm EDT are normally shipped the same business day.

    Accessory products shipping details vary depending on which store you are shopping in. Learn more.

    Free Shipping Offer

    We offer free shipping on online accessory orders over $50 within the continental US. However, in some cases certain items may require an additional shipping charge. If we anticipate any unexpected shipping charges we will contact you so you can decide if you would still like to place the order.

    Our US & Worldwide Store automatically selects a free shipping method, but you’ll typically still be able to choose between USPS and UPS Ground. This free shipping offer is not valid on hair system orders.
